Hospital Performance Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview
This project simulates a real-world hospital executive reporting system built using Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of financial performance, operational efficiency, and clinical quality indicators from 2020 to 2024.
The solution is structured as a multi-page executive reporting tool designed to replicate how hospital leadership monitors performance across departments and physicians.

Business Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze hospital performance across financial, operational, and clinical dimensions in order to:
Monitor revenue trends and department contribution
Track admission and readmission patterns
Evaluate operational efficiency using Average Length of Stay (LOS)
Assess physician performance using ranking metrics
Provide executive-level insights for decision-making

Dashboard Structure
1. Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of hospital performance, including:
Total Appointments
Total Admissions
Total Revenue
Readmission Rate
Revenue Contribution
Average Length of Stay
Includes time-series trends for:
Admissions by month
Readmission rate by month
Also includes department-level revenue and readmission comparison.

2. Financial Analysis
Focuses on revenue performance and contribution analysis:
Total Revenue by Year
Revenue trends over time
Revenue by Department
Revenue Contribution Percentage
Unpaid Revenue tracking
This page supports financial monitoring and strategic planning.

3. Clinical and Operational Performance
Analyzes operational efficiency and clinical outcomes:
Admissions by Department
Readmission Rate by Department
Average Length of Stay by Department
Doctor Performance Ranking
Doctor Performance Score
The doctor ranking model evaluates physician contribution using admissions and performance metrics.

Key KPIs
Total Revenue
Total Admissions
Revenue Growth
Revenue Contribution Percentage
Readmission Rate
Average Length of Stay (LOS)
Doctor Performance Rank
Data Modeling and Technical Implementation
Star schema data model
DAX measures for KPI calculation
Time intelligence for trend analysis
Performance ranking using DAX
Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering

Sample Insights
Cardiology contributes the highest share of total revenue.
Surgery demonstrates elevated readmission rates compared to other departments.
Revenue peaked in 2023 before declining in 2024.
Average Length of Stay remains stable at approximately 8 days.
Top-performing physicians were identified using ranking logic based on admissions and performance score.
